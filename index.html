About Me

My name is Sathvik, and I'm a business and data analyst with a passion for helping businesses explore, utilize, and visualize their data to drive decisions.

Throughout my career, I have experienced working in business, research, medical, engineering, and service-sector environments. Through these varied experiences, I have always been intrigued by the power of data and how many companies are either unable or unwilling to unleash this power to improve their business.

I believe that, with a little effort and a deep understanding of the available data, businesses can be taken from good to great.
